FAQ

LLM.txt generator

What is LLM.txt?

LLM.txt (sometimes llms.txt) is a small text file at your site root that lists priority URLs and guidance so AI crawlers understand what to cite. It complements—not replaces—your sitemap and on-page SEO.

Is LLM.txt the same as an XML sitemap?

No. A sitemap is the crawl inventory; LLM.txt is a human- and model-readable brief of what matters most. Many sites publish both.

Where should I host the generated file?

Upload it to your domain root as llm.txt or llms.txt so it is reachable at https://yourdomain.com/llm.txt, then confirm discovery with a technical audit.

Does this tool require an account?

No. The generator is free and runs without signup; it pulls structure from your public sitemap and key URLs.
