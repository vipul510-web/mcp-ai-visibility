About SellOnLLM — SEO for AEO & GEO

SellOnLLM is the leading platform for ranking and selling on AI platforms. We help brands optimize their presence on ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and other major AI platforms to get discovered by millions of AI-powered shoppers.

Our Mission

To empower businesses of all sizes to dominate the new frontier of AI search. As millions of consumers now rely on AI platforms for product recommendations and information, we believe every brand deserves to be visible when it matters most.

What We Do

SellOnLLM provides comprehensive tools and analytics to help you:

  • Track AI Traffic: Monitor how much traffic your store receives from ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and other AI platforms
  • Measure Conversions: Track whether your AI traffic is converting into actual sales
  • Improve Visibility: Get your AI brand visibility score and actionable recommendations to rank higher
  • Optimize Products: Compare your listings with ChatGPT's instant checkout requirements and get suggestions to improve
  • Audit Your Store: Run comprehensive LLM audits to identify gaps and opportunities for improvement

Why We Built This

Traditional SEO focuses on search engines like Google, but the future of discovery is changing. AI platforms like ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity are becoming the primary way people search for products and information. These platforms evaluate sources differently—focusing on authority, recency, citation networks, and contextual relevance.

We built SellOnLLM because we saw that businesses were missing out on this massive opportunity. While millions of consumers are already using AI for shopping recommendations, most brands don't know if they're being discovered or how to optimize for these platforms.
