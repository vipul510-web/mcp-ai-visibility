Why pair Google Analytics 4 with Search Console for SEO?

GA4 tells you what happens after the click—landing pages, engagement, conversions, and segments. Search Console tells you what happened before the click—queries, impressions, average position, and CTR. Together they explain whether a ranking problem is demand (impressions), relevance (CTR), or on-site experience (bounce, engagement, or conversion). That is the same signal stack strong AEO and GEO programs rely on: fix search fundamentals first, then invest in AI-surface experiments.

Technical SEO signals you can explore in chat

  • Queries losing impressions week over week (content decay or competition).
  • High-impression, low-CTR pages (title/meta or intent mismatch).
  • Landing pages with traffic but weak engagement (speed, UX, or content depth).
  • Device or country splits that suggest crawl or hreflang issues worth validating.

Frequently asked questions

What Google permissions does SellOnLLM request?
We use Google OAuth with read-only access to Google Analytics and Google Search Console so we can pull metrics, queries, and property lists. We do not ask for write access to your Analytics configuration or Search Console settings.
Do you store my Google password?
No. Authentication happens with Google. We keep an encrypted refresh token so sessions can refresh data until you disconnect or revoke access from your Google account.
Can I use Claude or ChatGPT?
Yes. Add an Anthropic or OpenAI API key in the sidebar. Our servers send your question plus aggregated GA4 and GSC context to that provider. You pay the LLM provider directly for usage.
How is this different from Looker Studio or the GA4 reports UI?
Dashboards help you explore; this product is built to compress exploration into decisions—narrative, hypotheses, and prioritized actions grounded in the same numbers, with follow-up questions in natural language.
Is this the same as the GA4 and Search Console MCP for Claude?
Same Google data sources, different integration: this page uses your LLM API key through our chat API. The MCP connector lets Claude.ai call tools over the Model Context Protocol with OAuth tokens for the MCP client.
How does this help AEO or GEO?
AI answers and generative results still lean on authoritative, crawlable pages with strong query performance. Fixing impressions, CTR, and on-page engagement improves the same pages models are likely to cite—so your AEO and GEO bets rest on measurable SEO, not vibes.
What if I only have GA4 or only Search Console?
You get the most value when both are connected. You can still sign in and select whichever source you have; the assistant will call out blind spots (for example, missing query data without GSC) so you do not over-fit to partial signals.
