Everything You Need to Know About LLM Marketing

What is LLM marketing and why does it matter?

LLM (Large Language Model) marketing is the practice of optimizing your brand's presence on AI platforms like ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity. As millions of users now rely on AI for product recommendations and information, appearing in these conversations is crucial. Studies show that 67% of AI users take action on recommendations from LLMs, making this the next frontier of digital marketing.

How does SellOnLLM track my brand across different AI platforms?

We use advanced AI monitoring technology to query major LLM platforms with thousands of relevant searches related to your industry and products. Our system tracks when your brand is mentioned, the context of mentions, sentiment, and positioning relative to competitors. We provide real-time alerts when significant changes occur in your visibility or rankings.

Can I see where my competitors rank on AI platforms?

Absolutely! Our competitor intelligence feature lets you track up to 10 competitors across all major LLM platforms. You'll see their ranking positions, share of voice, mention frequency, and sentiment scores. We also provide strategic insights on what's working for them and opportunities for you to gain competitive advantage.

How quickly can I expect to see results?

Most brands see improvements in LLM visibility within 4-6 weeks of implementing our recommendations. However, results vary based on your current authority, industry competitiveness, and implementation speed. Our customers typically see a 2-3x increase in LLM mentions within the first quarter, with continued growth over time.

How is this different from traditional SEO?

While traditional SEO optimizes for search engines like Google, LLM marketing optimizes for AI platforms that provide direct answers and recommendations. AI platforms evaluate sources differently—focusing on authority, recency, citation networks, and contextual relevance. SellOnLLM helps you excel in these new ranking factors while complementing your existing SEO strategy.
