SEO, AEO, GEO, and how SellOnLLM fits

What is AEO and why does technical SEO still matter?

AEO (answer-engine optimization) is about earning mentions and citations inside AI answers. Those answers still pull from crawlable web documents, schema, and authoritative queries—so technical SEO, internal linking, and Search Console hygiene remain prerequisites. SellOnLLM helps you prove the foundation with GA4 and GSC data before you scale creative experiments.

How does SellOnLLM use Google Analytics and Search Console for SEO?

Our web app connects with read-only OAuth to GA4 and verified Search Console properties. You (or your model) query trends, landing pages, queries, CTR, and device splits, then translate those signals into a prioritized backlog—meta fixes, internal links, content refreshes, and speed opportunities—before you spend budget on generative placements.

Can I see where my competitors rank on AI platforms?

Absolutely! Our competitor intelligence feature lets you track up to 10 competitors across all major LLM platforms. You'll see their ranking positions, share of voice, mention frequency, and sentiment scores. We also provide strategic insights on what's working for them and opportunities for you to gain competitive advantage.

How quickly can I expect to see results?

Most brands see improvements in LLM visibility within 4-6 weeks of implementing our recommendations. However, results vary based on your current authority, industry competitiveness, and implementation speed. Our customers typically see a 2-3x increase in LLM mentions within the first quarter, with continued growth over time.

How do AEO and GEO relate to traditional SEO?

Classic SEO earns clicks from Google results; AEO and GEO earn citations inside AI answers. The overlap is huge: crawlable HTML, accurate titles and schema, helpful on-page copy, authoritative backlinks, and strong query coverage still govern whether models trust you. SellOnLLM keeps you honest about the SEO foundation while you experiment with AI channels.
