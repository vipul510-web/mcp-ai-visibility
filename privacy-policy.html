Privacy Policy for GPT: Get Traffic from ChatGPT

Last Updated: October 6, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how GPT: Get Traffic from ChatGPT app ("we," "our," or "the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you install and use our Shopify application.

Information We Collect

1. Shopify Store Data

When you install our App, we access and process the following data from your Shopify store:

  • Product Information: Product titles, descriptions, prices, inventory quantities, variants, images, tags, product types, and categories
  • Product Metafields: Custom metafields (dimensions, color, size, gender, age group, size system) used for product feed analysis
  • Order Information: Order details including order IDs, creation dates, line items, pricing, and referrer URLs (used for LLM traffic analytics)
  • Shop Information: Store name, domain, and basic shop settings

Purpose: This data is used to analyze your products against ChatGPT's product feed requirements and provide recommendations for improving product data completeness.

2. Google Analytics 4 Data (Optional)

If you choose to connect your Google Analytics 4 account, we collect and store:

  • OAuth Tokens: Access tokens and refresh tokens to authenticate with Google Analytics
  • GA4 Property ID: The Google Analytics property you select for analytics tracking
  • Analytics Data: Session data, page views, traffic sources, and conversion events (read-only access)

Purpose: This data is used to show you analytics about LLM (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, etc.) traffic to your store and conversion rates.

3. App Usage Data

  • Session Data: Information about your use of the App interface
  • Error Logs: Technical logs to help us diagnose and fix issues

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  1. Analyze Product Data: Compare your product information against ChatGPT's product feed requirements
  2. Provide Recommendations: Identify missing or incomplete product fields and suggest improvements
  3. Track LLM Traffic: Show you analytics about traffic from AI platforms (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini)
  4. Calculate Conversions: Analyze conversion rates from LLM traffic sources
  5. Sync Data: Keep product and metafield information up-to-date through Shopify webhooks
  6. Improve the App: Use aggregated, anonymized data to improve app functionality

Data Storage and Security

  • Storage: All data is securely stored on Gadget.dev's infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit
  • Access Control: We implement role-based access control to ensure only authorized users can access their store data
  • Isolation: Your store's data is isolated from other stores' data through Gadget's multi-tenant architecture
  • API Security: All API communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption

Third-Party Services

Gadget.dev

We use Gadget.dev as our backend platform to host the app and store data. Gadget.dev complies with industry-standard security practices.

Google Analytics 4

If you connect GA4, we access your Google Analytics data using OAuth 2.0. We only request read-only access to analytics data and the ability to list your GA4 properties. Your Google credentials are never stored by us.

Shopify

The App operates within Shopify's ecosystem and follows Shopify's API guidelines and security requirements.

Data Sharing

We DO NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for purposes other than providing the App's functionality
  • Share your data with other Shopify stores or merchants

We MAY share data:

  • With Gadget.dev for hosting and infrastructure purposes
  • When required by law or legal process
  • In anonymized, aggregated form for analytics and research

Data Retention

  • Active Users: We retain your data while you have the App installed
  • Uninstalled App: When you uninstall the App, your data is retained for 30 days to allow for reinstallation, then permanently deleted
  • Analytics Data: GA4 analytics data is fetched in real-time and not permanently stored
  • Manual Deletion: You can request immediate data deletion by contacting us

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  1. Access: Request a copy of your data we have stored
  2. Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate data
  3. Deletion: Request deletion of your data (by uninstalling the App or contacting us)
  4. Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  5. Opt-Out: Disconnect GA4 integration at any time through the App settings

GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA):

  • We process data based on legitimate interest in providing the App's services
  • We comply with GDPR data protection requirements
  • We honor data subject rights including access, rectification, erasure, and portability
  • We handle GDPR requests through Shopify's GDPR webhooks

CCPA Compliance

For California residents:

  • We do not sell personal information
  • You have the right to know what personal information is collected
  • You have the right to request deletion of your personal information
  • You have the right to opt-out of data sales (though we don't sell data)

Cookies and Tracking

The App operates within Shopify's embedded app framework and does not set its own cookies. Session management is handled by Shopify's App Bridge.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by individuals under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Sending a notification through the App
  • Posting a notice in the App

App Permissions

The App requires the following Shopify permissions:

  • Read products: To analyze product data
  • Read product listings: To access published products
  • Read inventory: To check stock quantities
  • Read orders: To track LLM conversions (optional, for analytics feature)
  • Receive webhooks: To keep product data synchronized

Summary

What we collect: Product data, order data (for analytics), and optional GA4 analytics data

Why we collect it: To analyze product feed completeness and show LLM traffic analytics

Who we share it with: Only Gadget.dev (our hosting platform) and Google (if you connect GA4)

How long we keep it: While you use the App, plus 30 days after uninstall

Your rights: Access, correct, delete, or export your data at any time

This Privacy Policy is designed to be compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and Shopify's App Store requirements. However, we recommend having it reviewed by a legal professional before publishing.
