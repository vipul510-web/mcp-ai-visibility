About the Free LLM Audit

What does this audit check?

We scan your site for technical files (robots.txt, sitemap.xml, LLM.txt), metadata, structured data (JSON‑LD/Microdata/RDFa), content quality, mobile readiness, and more, then provide prioritized fixes.

Is it site‑wide or a single page?

Site‑wide by default. We crawl via sitemap or internal links and summarize page‑level findings with an overall score out of 100.

Do I need to sign up?

No sign‑up is required for the free audit. A deeper analysis and tracking require an account.

Can you generate missing files for me?

Yes. If LLM.txt, robots.txt, or sitemap.xml are missing, we provide ready‑to‑use templates directly in the report.
